Dearest Mom,


Words often feel inadequate to express the depth of my gratitude and love for you, but on this
Mother's Day, I want to try. More than just a day on the calendar, this is a moment to truly acknowledge the
incredible woman you are and the immeasurable impact you've had on my life.

Thinking back, I'm overwhelmed by the countless ways you've shaped me. You've been my constant
 source of strength, a pillar of unwavering support in every chapter of my journey. You celebrated my triumphs,
 no matter how small, and held my hand through the inevitable stumbles, offering wisdom and comfort when I needed
it most. You taught me the value of kindness, the importance of resilience, and the power of unwavering love.

You've always possessed a unique ability to see the best in me, even when I struggled to see it myself.
 Your belief in my potential has been a driving force, pushing me to pursue my dreams and strive for more than
 I thought possible. You've shown me what it means to be selfless, to put the needs of others before your own,
  and to love unconditionally.

Beyond the big moments, it's the little things that truly resonate. The warm hugs,
the comforting meals, the late-night talks, and the quiet moments of understanding. These seemingly
small gestures have woven a tapestry of love and security that has been the foundation of my life.

I am so grateful for your patience, your understanding, and your unwavering belief
in me. You've taught me how to navigate the complexities of life with grace and courage.
You've shown me what true love looks like, a love that is both fierce and gentle, unwavering and
unconditional.
Mom, you are more than just a mother; you are my confidante, my best friend, and my guiding
light. You are the embodiment of strength, love, and compassion. Today, and every day, I celebrate you.

Thank you for everything. I love you more than words can say. Happy Mother's Day.

With all my love,

Noor Hanafi
